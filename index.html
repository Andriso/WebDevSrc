Pretty fast after I placed the Polaroid Photo Viewer with CSS3 and jQuery example online, I received loads of e-mails and comments requesting another feature. The original example was only full-screen, but people wanted it to "stick" to a box (Placing it inside a <div>). That is exactly what this example does.

Try dragging the polaroids around. You'll be able to do so, but the surrounding division seems to prevent the user from actually dragging it outside it's container.

Do you want to see the surrounding division? Simply toggle the border!
